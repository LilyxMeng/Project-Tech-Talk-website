Application Process

To create a simple yet effective application process, we have implemented 3 stages to the procedure:

  1. Written Application
  2. Assignment
    Dome departments have assignments for you to complete. There, you can show us exactly what you can bring to Project Tech Talk! Submit your assignments in the written application form.
  3. Interview
    We may want to interview you to get to know you better! Don’t be nervous, it’ll simply be a casual conversation so we could better understand your ideas!
